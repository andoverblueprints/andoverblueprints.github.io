Also known as ACES, Andover Blueprints has focused on self-educating via engineering competitions since 2017.

The team typically consists of six members, who work year-round to prepare for their competitions.

Upcoming

In the fall of 2020, Andover Blueprints will compete in the virtual F1 in Schools North America competition. Though the competition is virtual this season, the team is excited to compete, since various logistical issues have impeded their ability to register for past years. The team has been working hard on the car design, pit booth, portfolio presentations, and other aspects of the competition, and plans to develop each component to its maximum potential this season.

Past

Andover Blueprints began their journey with Real World Design Challenge in 2016, winning first place in Massachusetts. In 2017, they won the national title. Ever interested in trying new things, the team sought out F1 in Schools. They advanced to nationals in 2018. Since 2018, the team has been unable to attend F1 in Schools due to timing with required exams, as well as the novel Coronavirus pandemic. However, years of hard work have kept the team sharp, and they are excited for the 2020 Nationals competition.
